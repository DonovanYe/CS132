Mango New York Cheesecake

The perfect creamy, tart cheesecake.

Servings: 8 to 10

Prep time: 30 minutes

Cook time:: 2 hours

Decorating Time:: 30 minutes

Total Time:: 3 hours

Ingredients

CRUST

FILLING

TOPPING

EQUIPMENT

  • Stand mixer or hand mixer
  • Blender or food processor
  • Deep roasting pan
  • 9-inch springform pan
  • 18-inch heavy-duty aluminum foil

Instructions

SET UP

  1. Follow the set up instructions for the Perfect Cheesecake Crust
  2. Start boiling a kettle of water.

THE CRUST

  1. Make the Perfect Cheesecake Crust

THE FILLING

  1. For a stand mixer, use the paddle attachment. Beat together room-temperature cream cheese, sugar, and flour for about 1 minute. Scrape the bottom and sides of the bowl as necessary.
  2. Peel and slice 2 mangos. Puree the mangos in the blender or food processor until smooth.
  3. Add the vanilla, mango puree, and salt into the bowl and mix on low until combined.
  4. Crack the eggs into a separate bowl. Add the eggs one at a time while mixing on low until each one is incorporated
  5. Mix in the sour cream and mix until the batter is uniform. Stop mixing immediately when the batter becomes uniform, making sure not to over-mix.

BAKING

  1. Transfer the filling to the springform pan and place the pan into the roasting tray.
  2. Fill the tray with hot water up to above an inch along the side of the pan.
  3. Bake the cake for about 1 hour 45 minutes to 2 hours until the cake is set. You will know that the cake is set when it jiggles together rather than rippling.
  4. Remove the cheesecake from the oven and let cook in the water bath for 45 minutes.
  5. Remove the cheesecake from the springform pan, using a thin knife along the sides if needed.
  6. Let cool in the fridge for 8 hours or overnight.

THE TOPPINGS

  1. Slice and peel the remaining mangos and puree with the sugar and water.
  2. Pour the puree over the cooled cheesecake, using a spatula to smooth along the top.
  3. Any remaining mango can be placed as whole fruit on the top. Optionally, a simple whipped frosting can be used to decorate the edges.
  4. Chill for an additional 30 minutes for the puree to set.

Serve and Enjoy!
